Game modes and high scores!

* Greatly simplifies the high scores, preferences, and many other aspects of Lexica, while also laying the foundations for future improvements.
* You can change game modes from the main screen, or create your own.
* High scores are based on game mode and lexicon.
* View your best scores by touching the high score on the main menu.
